About the App

The Vision

A high-contrast, minimalist news experience designed for focus. This is Version 1.0, focusing on a "Reel-style" delivery of global headlines with zero clutter.

How it Works

  • Curation: Content is aggregated via the NewsAPI.org engine.
  • Refresh Cycle: To ensure stability in this early build, the news feed refreshes every 4 hours.
  • Latency: Please note that due to upstream provider constraints, some articles may carry a delivery delay of up to 24 hours.

Terms & Transparency

  • Source Attribution: All news content, images, and headlines belong to their respective publishers. This app provides direct links to the original reporting for full context.
  • Data Usage: We do not track your reading habits or collect personal identification. We simply fetch the latest stories to your device.
  • Disclaimer: This service is provided "as-is." We are not responsible for the accuracy or opinions expressed in the linked third-party articles.
